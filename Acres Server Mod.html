ACRES SERVER MOD (WIP)

UNRELEASED - BETA 0.1

 

The 13 Acres of Hell Server Mod (Acres) is a (mostly) server side mod that combines and modified a number of other mods to provide a robust deathmatch experience. 

 

●     Based on the Reincarnation server side mod by Gen, Gonzo, and Bal.

○     Reincarnation provides a range of multi-player quality of life features and gameplay adjustments.  Most are basic (i.e. deathmatch 3 mode and power up drops), but there also some fun templay options and modes included.

○     Acres mod improves functionality and handling of rune mode

○     Acres mod improves functionality and handling of grappling hook (offhand hook added)

○     Acres mod adds weapon rebalancing (see notes below)

○     Acres mod fixes some minor bugs and issues in the original reincarnation mod.

●     Integrated Multiskin mod with my own curated selection of 32 player skins (see PDF chart showing off the skins)

●     Integrates the FrikBot mod, with bots using 16 of the new multi-skins.

●     This mod is intended to be used alongside a curated archive of deathmatch maps with pre-set and customizable map rotation support included. Frikbot waypoints are provided for most of the maps.

Server config: acresffa.cfg

---------------------------------------------

 

The acresffa.cfg has predefined gameplay settings for the server, but can of course be modified using the flags. 

 

Default server options in the acresffa.cfg file is as follows:

 

●     Weapons Stay: deathmatch 3 mode

●     Spawn with Armor (Green): players spawn with the green armor (100 armor)

●     Drop Powerups: players drop the quad/ring of shadows when killed if the timer isnt up

●     More Gibs!: more giblets flying around

●     Visible Weapon Drops: visible backpack weapons - drop your active weapon alongside your backpack, kinda like in Q2

●     Grappling Hook: grappling hook enabled (select axe twice or bind a key to +hook)

●     Rune Powerups: four runes (resistance, strength, haste, and regeneration) enabled

●     No Pentagram: pentagram of protection disabled

 

The acresffa.cfg provides a full list of server settings which can be configured. Note that many of the alternative game modes (teamplay modes, hunter + hunted, etc.) have not been extensively tested by me to ensure they are working properly. Verifying their functionality will be for another time!

Combat Balance Tweaks

=====================

 

******IMPORTANT********

Some of the weapon rebalancing changes below requires the server to set sv_maxvelocity 10000 to enable faster moving projectiles.

 

Quake has long suffered, IMHO, from some weakness when it comes to weapon balance in deathmatch. I absolutely adore quakes competitive DM mode - but let us not pretend its about anything other than the rocket launcher with a side of lightning.

 

A number of balance changes are implemented in this mod to make other weapons competitive against the rocket launcher and lightning gun while also refining the role of the weapon to make them all a little bit more distinctive in handling and capabilities.

 

Balance adjustments are as follows:

 

Axe

The axe was nearly useless. It now hits much harder and with greater reach, allowing it to land hits more consistently. Still a weapon for the desperate or insane.

 

●     Increased the axes reach by 100% (from 64 to 128 units)

●     Increased damage from 20 to 100

●     Increased fire rate to 0.35 from 0.50

 

Shotgun

Standard shotgun was modified with more damage and less spread to function more like a precision weapon.  While still outclassed by other weapons, at medium range it least has some degree of lethality.

 

●     Increased rate of fire to 0.4 from 0.5

●     Increased the number of pellets from 6 → 12 (4 damage per pellet, 48 max per hit)

●     Reduced spread from 0.4 to 0.01

 

Super Shotgun

The super shotgun now packs some punch, making it feel more in line with other FPS games where such shotguns are a joy to use.  Still has a moderate spread so its effectiveness at range drops off, but up close it packs a mighty punch.

 

●     Increased rate of fire to 0.6 from 0.7

●     Increased the number of pellets from 14 → 32 (4 damage per pellet, 128 max per hit)

●     Reduced spread from 0.14 / 0.08 → 0.09 / 0.07

 

Nailgun

The basic nail gun was a garbage weapon.  It still sort of is, but now at least the damage is much higher given its slower fire rate and projectile speed compared to the super nail gun. Has some utility at closer range if you can land your shots.

 

●     Damage increased from 9 to 27 (3x damage boost)

●     Projectile speed increased by 50%: from 1000 to 1500 units/s

 

Super Nailgun

This needed a buff big time.  The combination of high burn rate on ammo with only modest damage and slow projectiles was a hard sell.  Now this thing is closer to a minigun in handling (but still with projectiles), making it more distinct from normal nailgun and other weapons.

 

●     Increased rate of fire to 0.15 from 0.2

●     Increased projectile speed by 300%: from 1000 to 3000 units/s

●     Each shot only consumes 1 nail ammo now (instead of two)

●     Slightly reduced damage from 18 to 16 to compensate for other major buffs.

 

Grenade Launcher

The grenade launcher didnt really need a buff, but still felt like it could use something. To make it a more threatening area denial tool, the fire rate and damage has been upped..

 

●     Increased rate of fire to 0.5 from 0.6

●     Increased damage to 150 from 120

 

Rocket Launcher

●     No change

 

Lightning Gun

The lightning gun is a strong weapon, but the amount of ammo you get from pickups is just a tad too stingy for how quickly the weapon consumes ammo.  Tweaked slightly.

 

●     Increased the amount of ammo gained from cell packs by 50%

○     Small packs give 9 ammo (instead of 6)

○     Large packs give 18 ammo (instead of 12)

 

Armor

The Red Armor drives map control because of how strong it is.  While I dont want to completely change this aspect, the other armors (especially poor green) could REALLY use a boost. The armor damage reduction rates were adjusted as follow:

 

●     Green Armor   = Now 50% damage reduction ( from 30%), 100 armor max

●     Yellow Armor  = Now 65% damage reduction ( from 50%), 150 armor max

●     Red Armor      = 80% damage reduction (unchanged), 200 armor max

 

* Note that the default server settings for Acres uses a modified fullarmor setting, which means that players spawn with 100 green armor.

Grappling Hook

=====================

 

The original grappling hook in Reincarnation requires selecting the axe (impulse 1) twice, and then using the attack button.  This has been modified to ALSO work as an offhand grappling hook. Bind a key to +Hook (i.e. Bind Mouse2 +Hook) to enable the off-hand grappling hook.

 

=====================

Runes

=====================

 

Rune mode can be enabled through the server cvars. It is enabled by default in the acresffa.cfg file.

 

Four runes are spawned on the map and can be picked up by players.  Rune effects as follows:

 

●     Resistance rune = half damage taken

●     Strength rune = double damage dealt

●     Haste rune = increases attack speed for most weapons

●     Regeneration rune = regenerate health and armor up to 150. 

 

Runes can be dropped by binding a key to impulse 95 or droprune.

 

In order to drop a rune, you must stand on a different rune that you wish to pick up and then use the above binding.  Otherwise, the binding will inform you of the rune you are currently holding (in case you need a reminder!).

abinds.cfg

=====================

 

The abinds.cfg file allows you to edit your preferred keys for additional acres commands that might be useful.

 

impulse 95      =          drop your current rune (must stand on another rune for it to drop)

 

impulse 96      =          cycle to the next skin

impulse 97      =          cycle to the prior skin

 

impulse 60      =          add a random bot

impulse 62      =          remove a bot

 

+hook              =          use off-hand grappling hook

 

Impulse 98      =          weapon auto-switch enabled (starts disabled)
